# Phone Prop App — Director's Quick Guide

## Overview

This is a fake iOS phone interface running in a mobile browser. It contains:
- **Home Screen** — tap app icons to launch Messages or Tinder
- **Messages App** — 6 contacts with auto-reply scripts
- **Tinder App** ("Sparks 2026") — swipe cards, match animation, chat

All conversations have **auto-reply bots** built in. When the actor types a message and sends it, the contact will reply automatically after a short delay with a typing indicator.

---

## How to Launch

1. Open the URL on the iPhone in **Safari**
2. Tap **Share > Add to Home Screen** to make it full-screen (no browser chrome)
3. The system status bar (time, signal, battery) is handled by iOS — no duplicates

## Messages App — Auto-Reply Cheat Sheet

Tap the **Messages** icon on the home screen to open.

| Contact | Personality | Trigger Words | Example Replies |
|---------|------------|---------------|-----------------|
| **Emma** | Girlfriend, warm/romantic | Any message triggers a reply | "Hey you", "I love you too", "More than you know", "You're scaring me" |
| **Jake** | Best friend, casual | Any message | "Yooo", "Yeah I'm down", "What time?", "facts facts facts" |
| **Mom** | Caring, worried | Any message | "Hi sweetheart! How are you?", "I love you too sweetheart!", "Make sure you drink water!" |
| **Dad** | Estranged father | Any message | "Proud of you.", "We'll talk soon.", "Take care of yourself." |
| **Arjun** | Acquaintance | Any message | "cool", "will do", "noted" |
| **Agnes Lawyer** | Attorney, formal | Any message | "Call me at your earliest convenience.", "This is time sensitive." |

**Dad's thread** has 10 pre-loaded unanswered messages spanning several days — this is the emotional anchor scene. The actor does NOT need to type anything; just scroll to read.

### Suggested Demo Sequences

**Romantic scene (Emma):**
> Actor types "hey beautiful" → Emma replies "Hey you" → Actor types "i love you" → Emma replies "I love you too" + "More than you know"

**Dramatic scene (Emma):**
> Actor types "we need to talk" → Emma replies "Okay" → then "You're scaring me"

**Casual scene (Jake):**
> Actor types "yo" → Jake replies "Yooo" → Actor types "you around tonight?" → Jake replies "Yeah I'm down" + "What time?"

## Tinder App — Auto-Reply Cheat Sheet

Tap the **Tinder** icon on the home screen.

### Swiping

- **Swipe right** (or tap green heart) = Like
- **Swipe left** (or tap red X) = Nope
- **Olivia** (first profile) is set to **always match** on right-swipe
- After matching, a match animation plays automatically

### Olivia Chat (after matching)

Tap "Send Message" on the match screen, or go to Matches tab > Olivia.

| Actor Types | Olivia Replies With |
|-------------|-------------------|
| "hey" or anything | Random: "hey you", "finally someone with taste", "i could be free", etc. |
| "drink" or "coffee" | "i like how you think" or "know any good spots in soho?" |
| "dog" | "OMG you have a dog???" or "send pics immediately" |
| "cute" or "pretty" | "aww thanks" or "you're not so bad yourself" |
| "pizza" | "omg a man who gets it" or "wanna grab a slice at Joe's this weekend?" |

### Suggested Demo Sequence (Olivia)

> Actor types "hey" → Olivia replies "hey you" → Actor types "what are you up to this weekend?" → Olivia replies "i could be free" + "what did you have in mind?"

## Navigation Tips

- **Home indicator bar** (bottom line) — tap to return to home screen from any app
- **Back arrows** — tap to go back one level within an app
- Messages persist across navigation — leaving a chat and coming back preserves all messages
- The app remembers which screen you were on — re-tapping an app icon resumes where you left off

---

## Important Notes

- All replies are **randomized from a pool** — the exact wording may vary each take
- Reply delay is ~1.5–3 seconds with a visible typing indicator
- The app works **offline** — no internet needed after initial load
- If the app gets stuck, pull down to refresh the browser page
